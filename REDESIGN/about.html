My Story

I'm Leonardo Carôlo, a professional photographer specializing in automotive and motorsport photography. My journey began with a simple passion for capturing the beauty and energy of fast machines, but it has evolved into a comprehensive approach to visual storytelling.

With years of experience across multiple disciplines, I've developed a keen eye for detail, composition, and the perfect moment. Whether it's a high-speed track day, an exotic supercars gathering, or the peaceful beauty of the cosmos, each shot is crafted with intention and precision.
